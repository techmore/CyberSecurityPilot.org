Incident Reporting & Response

Emergency Contacts

  • NJ Cybersecurity (NJCCIC): 1-833-4-NJCCIC
  • PA-CSIRT: 1-866-800-0987
  • MS-ISAC: 1-866-787-4722

Reporting Procedures

  1. Document the incident details
  2. Contact your IT security team
  3. Report to state authorities:
    • NJ Incident Report Form
    • PA Cyber Incident Report
  4. Notify affected parties if required by law
